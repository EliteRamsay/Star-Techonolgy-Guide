How to get Hydrogen Gas

  1. Water Electrolysis

    For 1B (Bucket) of water in a Electrolyzer it will take 75 seconds and will output 2B of Hydrogen Gas and 1B of Oxygen Gas.

  2. Sodium Hydroxide Dust

    For 3 Sodium Hydrogen Dust in a Electrolyzer it will take 7.5 seconds and will output 1 Sodium Dust, 1B of Hydrogen Gas and 1B of Oxygen Gas.
