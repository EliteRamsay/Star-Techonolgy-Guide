How to get Oxygen

  1. Water Electrolysis

    For 1B (Bucket) of water in a Electrolyzer it will take 75 seconds and will output 2B of Hydrogen and 1B of Oxygen.

  2. Silicon Dioxide Electrolysis

    For 3 Silicon Dioxide Dust in a Electrolyzer it will take 3 seconds and will output 2B of Oxygen and 1 Silicon Dust.
